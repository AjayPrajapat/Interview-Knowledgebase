# Marketing Collaboration

## Overview

This category examines how BD partners with marketing on campaign quality, messaging, attribution, and funnel optimization.

## Why Interviewers Ask This

Interviewers test cross-functional maturity and shared ownership of pipeline outcomes.

## Key Concepts

- MQL/SQL alignment
- Campaign feedback loops
- Messaging consistency
- Attribution awareness
- SLA between teams

## Common Interview Questions

1. How do you align with marketing on lead quality?
2. What should a BD-marketing SLA include?
3. How do you give useful campaign feedback?
4. How do you align messaging across channels?
5. How do you coordinate ABM programs?
6. How do you handle attribution disagreements?
7. How do you improve conversion from marketing leads?
8. How do you collaborate on content strategy?
9. How do you run joint experiments?
10. Share an example of successful BD-marketing collaboration.

## Example Answers

- I establish shared definitions and funnel targets so both teams optimize for the same outcome.
- Feedback must be specific: lead source quality, persona fit, objection trends, and call outcomes.
- Joint experiments work best with clear hypotheses and review cadence.

## Real Business Scenarios

- A joint ABM initiative increased enterprise meetings after aligning outreach messaging with webinar themes.
- Tight SLA between marketing and BD reduced lead response time and lifted conversion.

## Best Practices

- Use a clear structure: context, action, outcome, learning.
- Quantify impact with metrics such as conversion rate, revenue, cycle time, or retention.
- Show buyer empathy and business understanding, not just sales tactics.
- Tie your answer to role expectations and company stage.
- Keep responses concise, then expand if asked.

## Mistakes to Avoid

- Giving generic answers without numbers.
- Over-focusing on activity volume instead of business outcomes.
- Criticizing past teams or customers.
- Ignoring cross-functional alignment with marketing, product, and customer success.
- Not asking clarifying questions in scenario prompts.
